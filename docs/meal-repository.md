# Meal Repository

This is the approved meal list for the Hebert household. Claude pulls from this list during weekly meal planning.

**How it grows:** During each weekly planning session, Claude proposes 10 new meals. Madison reviews and approves which ones get added here. Do not add meals to this document without explicit approval.

---

## How to Use During Planning

1. Pull from this list first when building the weekly menu
2. Prioritize variety — avoid repeating a meal within a 2-week window if possible
3. Identify protein anchors and build the week around them
4. Propose 10 new meals at the end of Section 2 for Madison to review
5. Add approved new meals to this file after the session

---

## Approved Meals

### Bowls

**Philly Cheesesteak Bowls**
- Bone broth rice (2 cups brown rice, 4 cups broth)
- Flank steak cut into strips, sautéed bell peppers
- Topped with provolone cheese

**Steak Bowls**
- Wild rice (2 cups rice, 4 cups water, cook ~45 min)
- Flank steak cut into strips
- Roasted butternut squash
- Topped with tahini

**Burger Buddha Bowls**
- Roasted sweet potatoes
- Ground beef cooked in tomato paste
- Topped with pickled red onions, pickles, tomatoes, cheese

**Sweet Potato Nachos**
- Sweet potatoes, black beans, rotisserie chicken or steak bites, corn, cheddar — baked on sheet pan
- Topped with tomatoes, tortilla chips, Greek yogurt, avocado

**Greek Bowls**
- Quinoa + ground turkey cooked in Greek seasoning
- Feta, cucumber, tzatziki, pickled red onions

**Salmon Bowls**
- Coconut rice (2 cups rice + 1 can coconut milk + water to make 4 cups total liquid + 2 tsp coconut sugar)
- Baked salmon
- Cooked broccoli, mushrooms, and sweet potato
- Topped with coconut aminos

**Street Corn Bowls**
- Bone broth rice
- Corn (canned or fresh) cooked on skillet, mixed with cottage cheese, mayo, tajin, cotija cheese, salt, lime juice
- Ground beef or chicken nuggets
- Topped with avocado and red onion

---

### Sheet Pan

**Sheet Pan**
- Shrimp, chicken sausage, sheet pan veggies baked together

**Salmon Sheet Pan**
- Salmon topped with lemon slices
- Baby potatoes, asparagus roasted together
- Topped with feta and Greek goddess dressing after baking

---

### Pasta & Casseroles

**Messy Lasagna**
- Rao tomato sauce + Banza noodles + ground beef + cottage cheese
- Zucchini mixed in OR broccoli on the side

**Easy Chicken Marinara**
- Chicken nuggets + Rao sauce + topped with mozzarella
- Vegetable on the side

**Kids Mac & Cheese**
- 12 oz macaroni cooked in water (don't strain)
- Add 1 can evaporated milk + 12 oz shredded cheddar in saucepan
- Salad on side

**Butternut Squash Mac & Cheese**
- 1 roasted butternut squash (1 hr at 400°) blended with chicken broth, salt, and nutritional yeast
- Tossed with cooked noodles and broccoli, topped with cheddar, baked in casserole dish
- Can add rotisserie chicken

---

### Soups & Stews

**Taco Soup**
- Corn, beans (canned or frozen), rotisserie chicken, canned chilis
- Topped with tortilla chips, cheese, plain Greek yogurt, lime

**Coconut Curry**
- Chicken marinated in Greek yogurt and curry powder
- Onions, carrots, sweet potato, coconut milk, turmeric — cooked in saucepan
- Add chickpeas and/or bell peppers if available
- Naan bread on side

---

### Sandwiches & Light

**BLTAs**
- Bacon, lettuce, tomato, avocado, mayo
- Fruit on side

**Chicken Salad**
- Rotisserie chicken + mayo + celery + grapes + salt & pepper
- Fruit on side (watermelon, pineapple, etc.)

---

### Breakfast (for dinner or morning)

**Kodiak Pancakes**
- Blend in cottage cheese
- Breakfast sausage + fruit on side

- **Protein anchors this family uses:** flank steak, ground beef, ground turkey, rotisserie chicken, salmon, shrimp, chicken nuggets, chicken sausage

- **Rice defaults:** brown rice with bone broth, wild rice (45 min cook), coconut rice
